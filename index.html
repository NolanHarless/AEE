Our History

Aircraft Electrical Electronics, an independent family-owned and operated repair station, has been doing business in the aviation industry since the early 1980’s. In that time, we’ve developed a reputation for economical prices, short turn times, and quality workmanship. We take pride in the long-standing customer relationships we’ve cultivated through years of hard work, dedication, honesty, and ethical business integrity.

How Our Services Can Benefit You

Instead of purchasing replacement components or sending them directly to the manufacturer for electrical issues, let us rewind and repair those to Serviceable condition at a fraction of the cost. Our repairs come certified with FAA Form 8130-3 and are guaranteed for a 1 year warranty period. In AOG situations, we offer an expedite service where parts can be repaired and shipped out in 2—3 days. We promise our customers good pricing, quick turn times, and impeccable workmanship.

Let's work together.

Aircraft Electrical Electronics specializes in small to miniature electrical sub-components, but has capability and experience for all size stators, rotors, fields, armatures, solenoids, and coils. Our range can extend from Motors .001 HP and smaller, generators up to as large as 150 KW. Essentially….if it has magnet wire in it, we can rewind it! This facility is also certified to balance rotables (rotors, armatures, fans, etc…). End use of our repairs encompasses all aviation from commercial to military to corporate, for both fixed-wing and rotorcraft aircraft.
