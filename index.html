Our History

Aircraft Electrical Electronics, an independent family-owned and operated repair station, has been doing business in the aviation industry since the early 1980’s. In that time, we’ve developed a reputation for economical prices, short turn times, and quality workmanship. We take pride in the long -standing customer relationships we’ve cultivated through years of hard work, dedication, honesty, and ethical business integrity.
